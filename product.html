Q. Can I use the 10% Niacinamide Serum if I have normal skin? How will it be beneficial?

Yes. You can use our 10% Niacinamide+Cica Serum for normal skin as well. Even though the serum has been curated to treat acne, excess oil & pigmentation, for normal skin like yours, it will only further refine your skin texture & make it appear healthier.

Q. How does 10% Niacinamide serum visibly minimize the pores?

A. Over the years of research, Niacinamide is clinically proven to minimize the appearance of pores. Our 10% Niacinamide serum has been curated to control excess oil with 2x efficacy. The serum penetrates deep to unclog pores of excess oil, thereby eventually reducing the appearance of pores when used regularly, twice a day. It restores pore functioning, soothes skin & improves overall skin texture.

Q. Is 10% Niacinamide+Cica serum suitable for sensitive skin?

A. Yes! Our serum contains ingredients like Cica, Niacinamide & 3% Tranexamic Acid, all of which come together to control acne breakouts and soothe irritation, redness & skin inflammation, making it great for sensitive skin as well.

Q. What's the benefit of 3% Tranexamic in the serum?

A. The concentration of 3% Tranexamic in this serum boosts the bioavailability of Niacinamide which further makes this effective against concerns like hyperpigmentation, dark spots & pore appearance.
